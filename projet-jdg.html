About The Competition

Description

The Engineeing Games are a annual compilation of many competitions between 12 universities from all arround Quebec. For my part, I was involved in the challenge Machine, the challenge Major and the electrical academic challenge.

Description of the Machine

The Machine Challenge is a robotic design challenge that lasts 4 months. The 2023 challenge required the construction of a robot capable of connecting and crossing a set of islands thanks to bridges scattered on the ground.


Description of the Majeur

The Major Challenge is a robot design challenge that lasts 8 hours. The 2023 challenge required the construction of a robot capable of placing tickets in various locations on a field. For the construction of the robot, the same limited material is provided to all teams.


Description of the Electrical Acad.

The academic challenge is composed of two segments that last 4 hours. A theoretical team that must manage to answer as many questions as possible in a theoretical exam. The second team of two takes care of the practical aspect of the challenge. This challenge involves making an electrical circuit.
In 2023, I was part of the practical team and the challenge was to make a multimeter with the equipment provided. That year I got first place for the practical segment of the challenge.

My role

During this competition, we were 36 persons representing the ETS. Of this group, I was the lead of the electrical and software team for the Machine challenge. My role for the Major challenge was to design and build the circuit of the prototype.
